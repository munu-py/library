About Us

Sharing books makes reading easier. We have created a platform where you can come forward and share and borrow books with one and all, making this planet a slightly better place to live in.

When trees are cut down, their stored carbon is released into the air as carbon dioxide. Deforestation is responsible for about 10 percentage of all global warming emissions. Let's come together and share our books and knowledge with each other. Because we are here to help you with it. Upload books you are willing to share, choose books you are going to borrow and we will provide pickup and delivery at your doorstep.
